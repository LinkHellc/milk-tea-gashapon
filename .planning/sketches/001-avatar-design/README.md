# Sketch 001: Avatar Design

## Design Question
What avatar style best represents the milk tea gashapon concept and fits WeChat Mini Program icon requirements?

## How to View
Open `.planning/sketches/001-avatar-design/index.html`

## Variants
- **A: Cup Gashapon** — Milk tea cup shaped as gashapon machine, cute cartoon style
- **B: Bubble Character** — Personified bubble tea with bouncy expressions
- **C: Capsule Focus** — Transparent capsule as hero element with gradient fill

## What to Look For
- Clarity at small sizes (WeChat avatar is 32x32px in lists)
- Brand recognition as milk tea gashapon
- Playful and inviting feel
- Works as app icon (square with rounded corners)
